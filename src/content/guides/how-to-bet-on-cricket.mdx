# How to Bet on Cricket

**Complete Step-by-Step Guide (Pre-match & Live Betting)**

Betting on cricket involves more than simply picking a winner. Modern cricket betting platforms offer multiple providers, various market types, different odds formats, and both pre-match and live betting options. Understanding the complete process—from logging in to confirming your bet—helps ensure you're placing bets correctly and understanding what you're betting on.

## 1. Navigating to the Sports Section
To begin your cricket betting journey, you first need to access the sports betting providers.

1.  **Log In:** Ensure you are logged into your account.
2.  **Top Menu:** Click on the "Sports" or "Exchange" tab located in the main navigation menu at the top of the homepage.
3.  **Select Provider:** Choose your preferred sports betting provider (e.g., SBO, IBC, or the Exchange).

## 2. Finding Cricket Matches
Once you're inside the sportsbook interface, locating cricket matches is straightforward.

*   **Left Sidebar:** Look at the left-hand sidebar which lists all available sports.
*   **Select Cricket:** Click on "Cricket" to filter the view.
*   **Filter by Time:** You will often see tabs for "Live" (matches currently being played), "Today" (matches starting today), or "Early" (matches in the coming days).
*   **Select League/Tournament:** Click on the specific tournament (e.g., IPL, BPL, The Ashes) to see the individual matches.

## 3. Understanding the Odds
Before placing a bet, it's crucial to understand how odds work.

*   **Decimal Odds (Default):** This is the most common format (e.g., 1.85). If you bet 100 BDT at 1.85, your total return is 185 BDT (85 BDT profit + 100 BDT stake).
*   **Favorites vs. Underdogs:** The team with the lower odds is the favorite (expected to win), while the team with higher odds is the underdog.

## 4. Placing a Pre-Match Bet
Pre-match betting means placing a wager before the first ball is bowled.

1.  **Select Market:** Click on the match you want to bet on. You will see various markets (Match Winner, Top Batsman, Total Runs, etc.).
2.  **Click the Odds:** Click on the odds corresponding to the outcome you predict.
3.  **Bet Slip:** The selection will automatically be added to your "Bet Slip," usually located on the right side of the screen.
4.  **Enter Stake:** Enter the amount you wish to wager in the bet slip.
5.  **Confirm:** Review your potential winnings and click "Place Bet."

## 5. Placing a Live (In-Play) Bet
Live betting allows you to place bets while the match is happening.

1.  **Go to Live Section:** Select the "Live" tab within the cricket section.
2.  **Watch the Odds:** Odds change rapidly during a live match based on the action (e.g., a wicket falls, a boundary is hit).
3.  **Quick Action:** Click the odds for your desired outcome.
4.  **Confirm Quickly:** Enter your stake and click "Place Bet" immediately, as odds may change and lock your bet if you wait too long.

> **Tip:** Live betting requires quick decision-making and a good understanding of the game's momentum.
